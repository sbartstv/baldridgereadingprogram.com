Privacy Policy

How the Baldridge Reading and Study Strategy Program collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: June 2026. This policy applies to BaldridgeReadingProgram.com and all related enrollment, contact, and consultation forms.

Information We Collect

When you contact us, request a free consultation, or enroll in the program, we may collect the following information directly from you (the parent or adult site visitor):

  • Name and email address
  • Phone number, if provided
  • Student grade level and school information you choose to share
  • Messages or details submitted through our contact form
  • Payment information processed securely through Stripe at the time of enrollment (we do not store your card details ourselves)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to respond to consultation requests, schedule and deliver the program, process enrollment payments, and send you information about your sessions. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Children's Privacy

This website and its enrollment forms are directed at parents and other adults, not at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age of 13. Any student information referenced on our forms (such as grade level) is submitted by a parent or guardian on the student's behalf, not by the child.

If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us directly, please contact us using the information below and we will promptly remove it.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate this site, including Stripe for payment processing and a form delivery service for contact form submissions. These providers have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data on our behalf.

Data Retention & Security

We retain contact and enrollment information only as long as needed to provide our services and meet recordkeeping obligations. We take reasonable steps to protect your information, though no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us directly.

Accessibility

This site is built to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA accessibility standards. If you experience any difficulty accessing content on this site, please let us know so we can assist you and address the issue.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.

Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy or your information? Reach out:

Visit our Contact page or email us directly.
